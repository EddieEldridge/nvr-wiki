## Main

### Brightness

>Default: 1.1

### Saturation

 Overall image saturation.

>Default: 0.7

### Contrast

 Overall image contrast.

>Default: 1.2

### Strength

 Strength of the effect.

>Default: 1.0

### DarkColorR

>Default: 0.9

>Default: 0.7

>Default: 0.7

>Default: 1.0

>Default: 1.0

>Default: 1.0
