# HDR

---

## Main

### BloomExponent

makes the bloom more spread out or more focused

>Default: 1.0

---

### BloomStrength

scales the game's bloom pass strength

>Default: 2.1

---

### Exposure

scales the lighting strength before tonemap

>Default: 1.1

---

### Gamma

gamma modifier to be applied after tonemapping

>Default: 2.2

---

### Linearization

>Default: 2.2

---

### PointLightMultiplier

Experimental - modifies the strength of all point lights

>Default: 1.0

---

### Saturation

Saturation of the image before tonemapping

>Default: 1.0

---

### SkyMultiplier

Scales the brightness of the sky and sky textures. Requires Sky shader enabled

>Default: 1.3

---

### ToneMapping

Unused

>Default: 1.0

---

### ToneMappingBlur

Unused

>Default: 0.0

---

### ToneMappingColor

Unused

>Default: 1.2

---

### TonemappingMode

changes the tonemapping algorithm. 0: game native, 1:ACES, 2:Reinhard, 3: Lotte, 4:Uncharted2

>Default: 3

---

### WhitePoint

White point setting for Reinhard tonemapper. Changes the value of the brightest pixels

>Default: 100.0

---

### LotteContrast

Contrast setting for Lotte Tonemapper

>Default: 5.9

---

### LotteBrightness

Brightness setting for Lotte Tonemapper

>Default: 0.5

---

### LotteMidpoint

Midpoint setting for Lotte Tonemapper

>Default: 1.2

---

### LotteShoulder

Curve shape towards white point for Lotte Tonemapper

>Default: 0.5

---
