# WetWorld

## Main

### Amount

Darkness of puddles

>Default: 1.0

---

### Decrease

Time it takes in game hours for puddles to dissipate

>Default: 1.2

---

### Increase

Time it takes in game hours for puddles to appear

>Default: 0.3

---

---

Not used.

>Default: 0.4

### Enabled

Water ripples on surfaces, and puddles based on physical terrain pockets during rainy weathers.

>Default: true
